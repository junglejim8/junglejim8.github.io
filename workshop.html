Workshop 2017

Week 1: May 13-20
   -Timber Framing

Week 2: May 21-28
   -Earthen Plasters

Details

Join us in Montana, this May, for a Natural Building Workshop. This two week event will be packed full of hands on learning experience. Camp up on the labs and eat with the other participants. We will have the materials gathered so that later stages of construction can be experienced too. Apprentices may come 2 weeks early.

  • Roundwood timber framing
  • Earth Bermed structures
  • Green/Sod roofs
  • Slip-straw walls
  • Cob plasters
  • Slip and chip walls
  • Two story construction
  • Saw Mill operation

Campsite and groceries included. We will provide an outdoor kitchen setup with essential organic food ingredients for the workshopers to cook.

**Early Bird Pricing** $600 for both weeks. Or $350 for any 7 days.
Price after March 1: $800 for both weeks. Or $450 for any 7 days.
Worktrade: $85, must come 2 weeks early to prep materials

Schedule:
Week 1 (Timber framing and structures)
May 13: Arrival, camp setup, and tours

May 15: materials selection, how to fell a tree, peel a log
May 16: How to prepare the site, earth moving, drainage design
May 17: Putting posts up
May 18: Joining Beams
May 19: Green Roofs
